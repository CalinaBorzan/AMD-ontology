A prospective, randomized, masked and controlled trial of intravitreal bevacizumab Avastin versus verteporfin photodynamic therapy PDT in patients with  neovascular age-related macular degeneration. Age-related macular degeneration AMD  is the leading cause of visual impairment and social blindness in people 60 years or older.  Approximately 75 of patients with severe visual loss exhibit neovascular AMD with choroidal neovascularization CNV. Newly formed vessels invade the retinal pigment epithelium RPE and neural retina, causing edema and hemorrhage, ultimately inducing damage or apoptotic cell death of photoreceptors.
